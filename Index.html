Welcome to OpenSkiAI

A community-driven project combining skiing, technology, and open collaboration.

Our Ethos

At OpenSkiAI, we believe in harnessing the power of technology and collaboration to make skiing accessible, fun, and innovative for everyone. We operate on three key principles:

  • What We Know: Sharing knowledge about skiing, motion tracking, and AI freely and openly.
  • What Is Free: Using open-source tools and hardware to empower anyone to join the project.
  • What Is Available: Repurposing accessible technology (like Kinect sensors and AI tools) to innovate.

This project is a living, breathing example of open collaboration—combining community knowledge, creativity, and technology to bring ideas to life.
